Player Unknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG) is a buy to play online multiplayer battle royale game where up to one hundred players can join into a single game to have a “battle royale” experience. PUBG promotes a player versus player model and the game is developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of the South Korean video game company Bluehole.

TThe game was originally based on the “Battle Royale” film, and was first developed as a mod for other games, before being published and released as PUBG in December 2017. Originally it was available on Windows, through the video game distribution service Steam, and Xbox One via Xbox Game Preview; but was later also released on other platforms due to its popularity. Players around the world can also enjoy it on PlayStation 4 and free to play version on mobile, for Android and iOS users alike.
